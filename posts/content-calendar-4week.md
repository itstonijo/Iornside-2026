## VARIATIONS & FLEXIBILITY

### If a post underperforms:
- Don't panic – one post doesn't make the strategy
- Post the same topic again in different format 2 weeks later
- Example: If "What would you do with 90%?" gets 1.5k impressions, post a carousel version 2 weeks later

### If you get a sudden spike:
- Capitalize on it
- Post related content ASAP
- Engage heavily on the viral post
- Redirect momentum to strategy call bookings

### If current events are relevant:
- Pivot a post to address news/trends
- Example: If there's a major recruitment industry news item, create a hot-take post

### If an operator wins big:
- Pause calendar, do immediate case study post
- Interrupt schedule for social proof gold
