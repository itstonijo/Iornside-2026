# LinkedIn Post Launch Checklist

## PRE-POSTING (24 hours before)

- [ ] **Copy review**: Read aloud 2x. Check for typos, clarity, flow.
- [ ] **Link check**: Verify strategy call URL is correct and tracking parameters work
  - `https://www.xrecruiter.io/strategy-call?utm_source=linkedin&utm_medium=social&utm_campaign=qeh-recruitment&utm_content=july-post`
- [ ] **Visual assets**: Download/finalize graphics (1200x627px minimum)
- [ ] **Hashtag review**: Ensure 8-12 relevant hashtags selected
- [ ] **Preview**: Post preview on desktop and mobile (use LinkedIn's draft feature)
- [ ] **Timing**: Confirm 8:00 AM AEST Tuesday, July 7 posting time
- [ ] **Engagement team**: Brief any team members who will help respond to comments

---

## AT POST TIME (8:00 AM AEST, Tuesday, July 7)

### Step 1: Post Creation (5 min)
1. Go to LinkedIn (desktop preferred)
2. Click "Start a post"
3. Copy paste the post copy
4. Upload visual asset
5. Add hashtags
6. Set posting time OR post immediately
7. Review preview one final time
8. **POST**

### Step 2: Immediate Engagement (First 30 minutes)

- [ ] **Set reminder**: 30 min after post goes live
- [ ] **Like your own post** (boosts algorithm visibility)
- [ ] **Reply to your own post** with value-add comment:
  - Add a stat, insight, or follow-up question
  - This keeps conversation going early
  - Example: "BTW - this assumes you already have clients. If you're starting from zero, our onboarding is built for that too. The difference? You still keep 90% once you build it."
  
- [ ] **Monitor early reactions**: Watch for first 50 interactions, respond to all comments within 30 min

### Step 3: First 2 Hours (Ongoing)

- [ ] **Reply to EVERY comment** within 2 hours of posting
  - Answer questions directly
  - Ask follow-up questions to keep conversation going
  - Don't sell, just engage genuinely
  - Example exchanges:
    - Comment: "Sounds great but what about compliance?"
    - Reply: "Great question. Compliance, contracts, invoicing, debt collection — we handle all of it. That's why you keep 90% and not 70-80%. Let's talk through your specific concerns on a call: [LINK]"

- [ ] **Engage with other posters' content**
  - Find 10-15 posts in your network about recruitment, business, entrepreneurship
  - Comment genuinely (add value, don't spam)
  - This boosts your profile visibility & algorithm favor

### Step 4: 4-8 Hours After Posting

- [ ] **Check analytics**: LinkedIn will show impressions, clicks, engagement
- [ ] **Respond to new comments** (every 2-4 hours for 24 hours)
- [ ] **Share post to relevant groups** (if you're a member):
  - Recruitment leaders groups
  - Entrepreneur/startup groups
  - Australian business groups
  - Solo recruiter communities

### Step 5: 24 Hours After Posting

- [ ] **Post a "boost" comment**: Add a new insight or stat
  - Example: "Update: This post hit 5,000 impressions in 24 hours. Clearly this resonates. If you're considering it, the next intake for Agency in 90 Days is [DATE]. 3 spots left. Book: [LINK]"

## ENGAGEMENT BEST PRACTICES

### DO:
✅ **Respond within 2 hours** to every comment
✅ **Answer questions specifically** – show expertise
✅ **Ask follow-up questions** – keep conversation alive
✅ **Use names** when replying ("Thanks for the question, [Name]...")
✅ **Share personal stories** when relevant
✅ **Link to relevant resources** (LinkedIn articles, case studies, strategy call booking)
✅ **Thank people for adding value** – builds community

### DON'T:
❌ Use generic responses ("Great question!")
❌ Oversell or hard-pitch
❌ Ignore controversial or challenging comments
❌ Post and ghost – engagement is a 7-day game
❌ Reply to spam/bot comments
❌ Get defensive if criticized

### HANDLING OBJECTIONS/PUSHBACK

**Comment**: "This is a pyramid scheme"
**Reply**: "Not at all – we don't make money from recruiting people into the program. We make money from the operator platform fees. You succeed financially, we succeed. No MLM element. Happy to clarify on a call: [LINK]"

**Comment**: "What's the catch?"
**Reply**: "The catch is that you need to have existing clients/relationships. We're not building your pipeline for you. You bring the relationships, we remove the admin + provide the playbook. Does that make sense?"

**Comment**: "Too good to be true"
**Reply**: "Totally fair skepticism. The numbers work because: 1) You're already a proven biller, 2) Your clients want direct access to you (not to a big agency), 3) Tech that used to cost $10k/mo is now accessible as a platform. Want to pressure-test the numbers? 30-min call: [LINK]"

## POSTING OPTIMIZATION SIGNALS

### Post Performs Well If:
- Engagement rate > 3% (likes + comments + shares / impressions)
- Comments within first hour = 5+
- Shares within first 24 hours = 2+
- Profile visits spike during/after posting
- Strategy call bookings come from post traffic (check UTM params)

### If Post Underperforms:
- Review: Was timing off? Wrong day/time?
- Review: Did visual asset load properly?
- Review: Was CTA clear enough?
- Don't delete – leave it up. Older posts sometimes attract new attention.
- Plan: What worked in other high-performing posts? Replicate format next time.
