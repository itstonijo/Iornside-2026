Fewer, larger agencies. Less opportunity for individual recruiters to grow. Less differentiation in service.

You bill $100k. Your company keeps $80-85k. Inflation eats the rest. You're working harder for less.

Higher margins. Faster decisions. Better client service. Stronger reputation = stronger brand loyalty.
