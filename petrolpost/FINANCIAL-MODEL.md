# Financial Model — Revhead Post

*All figures in AUD, illustrative, based on July 2026 costs. Replace with your
own quotes before relying on them. Assumes sole trader, not GST-registered
(turnover under $75k), so no GST is added or claimed.*

---

## 1. Cost to produce one drop (COGS)

Estimated per-unit cost at **low volume** (first few hundred boxes). Costs drop
meaningfully at scale — get real quotes from the printers in
[SUPPLIERS-AND-TOOLS.md](SUPPLIERS-AND-TOOLS.md).

| Item | Low-volume unit cost |
|------|----------------------|
| Rigid/board-backed envelope or mailer | $0.55 |
| 3 × Polaroid-style photo prints | $0.50 |
| 1 × A5 artwork print | $0.55 |
| 1 × A5 blueprint print | $0.45 |
| 1 × trading card (card stock) | $0.35 |
| 1 × printed story letter | $0.15 |
| Branded sticker / insert / thank-you | $0.25 |
| **Materials subtotal** | **≈ $2.80** |
| Australia Post — Large Letter (domestic) | ≈ $2.20 |
| **Total COGS + postage per box** | **≈ $5.00** |

> Keeping the drop inside the **Large Letter** band (≤125g, ≤20mm thick) is what
> keeps postage at ~$2 instead of ~$10+ for a parcel. Design to that constraint.

## 2. Unit economics per subscriber (monthly tier @ $19.90)

| Line | Amount |
|------|--------|
| Price | **$19.90** |
| Payment processing (~1.75% + $0.30) | −$0.65 |
| COGS + postage | −$5.00 |
| **Contribution margin per box** | **≈ $14.25 (≈ 72%)** |

Annual prepaid ($199) improves cash flow and cuts payment fees to one
transaction: contribution ≈ **$193 over 12 boxes ≈ $16.10/box** after COGS.

---

## 4. Break-even

- Contribution per subscriber ≈ **$14.25/mo**.
- To cover ~**$115/mo** of fixed overhead: **≈ 9 subscribers**.
- To also pay yourself, say, **$3,000/mo**: you need
  ($3,000 + $115) ÷ $14.25 ≈ **≈ 220 subscribers**.
- To reach **$5,000/mo profit**: ≈ **360 subscribers**.

These are very achievable subscriber numbers for a niche with Australia's
car-culture size — the challenge is acquisition and retention, not the maths.

## 6. Levers that most improve the numbers

1. **Push annual prepay** — better cash flow, lower churn, fewer payment fees.
2. **Cut COGS at scale** — bulk print runs and negotiated rates can nearly halve
   materials cost past a few hundred units.
3. **Raise average order value** — a $29 "collector" tier with an enamel pin or
   diecast, plus back-issue and framed-print sales.
4. **Reduce churn** — the trading-card *set* and "don't break your collection"
   psychology is your best retention tool.
5. **Keep postage in the Large-Letter band** — the difference between a letter
   and a parcel is the difference between a healthy and a broken margin.
