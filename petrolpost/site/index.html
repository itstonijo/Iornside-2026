Questions

Do I get to choose the car?

No — that's the whole point. Every drop is a surprise built around one legendary car. Founders help vote on future cars once you're subscribed.

When does it ship?

Every drop lodges with Australia Post on the 1st of the month, so it lands in your letterbox in the first few days.

Can I cancel?

Anytime, straight from your account. Monthly plans stop at the end of your current cycle — no lock-in, no phone calls.

Is this an official manufacturer product?

No. Revhead Post is an independent car-culture publication and is not affiliated with or endorsed by any vehicle manufacturer. We create our own original artwork and licensed or original photography.

Do you ship outside Australia?

We're launching Australia-wide first. International shipping is on the roadmap — join the waitlist to hear when it opens.
