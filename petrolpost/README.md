# Revhead Post — Australian Car-Culture Subscription Box

> A monthly "snail-mail club" for Australian car enthusiasts. Each envelope is a
> surprise built around one legendary car — Polaroid-style photos, an artwork
> print, a blueprint, a collector trading card, and a letter telling its story.
> Modelled on the UK's [Petrol Post](https://petrolpost.com), rebuilt for the
> Australian market and legal environment.

**Working brand name:** *Revhead Post* (placeholder — see
[LEGAL-AND-IP.md](LEGAL-AND-IP.md) before you commit to it and check
domain + trademark availability).

## Start here — the 10-day launch path

You do **not** need much money or a company to start. The fastest legal route is:
**sole trader + ABN (free) → register a business name ($47) → build a Shopify
store → line up a printer → soft-launch to a waitlist.** Total cash to be
"legally trading" in Australia: **under $150.**

| # | Do this | Where | Cost | Time |
|---|---------|-------|------|------|
| 1 | Read the plan + the IP risks | This folder | — | 1 hr |
| 2 | Apply for a free ABN as a sole trader | [business.gov.au](https://business.gov.au/registrations/register-for-an-australian-business-number-abn) | $0 | 15 min |
| 3 | Register your business name | [ASIC via business.gov.au](https://business.gov.au/registrations/register-your-business-name) | $47/yr | 20 min |
| 4 | Buy the domain (.com.au needs an ABN) | [auDA registrar](https://www.auda.org.au) | ~$20/yr | 15 min |
| 5 | Open a separate business bank account | Any AU bank | $0 | 30 min |
| 6 | Build the store + subscription | [Shopify AU](https://www.shopify.com/au) | ~$51/mo | 1 day |
| 7 | Get sample prints from a printer | See [SUPPLIERS](SUPPLIERS-AND-TOOLS.md) | ~$50 | 3–5 days |
| 8 | Publish the landing page + collect a waitlist | `site/` in this folder | $0 | 1 hr |
| 9 | Photograph/design the first 3 "drops" | You / Canva | $0 | 2–3 days |
| 10 | Open pre-orders to the waitlist | Shopify | — | — |

Full step-by-step with official links: **[STARTUP-CHECKLIST.md](STARTUP-CHECKLIST.md)**

## The one thing that will make or break this

**Image and trademark rights.** Petrol Post sells prints and photos of specific
cars (Ferrari, Porsche, Lamborghini, etc.). Those brands, badges, and many
photographs are protected by trademark and copyright. Building a business on
imagery you don't own or licence is the single biggest risk here. The whole of
[LEGAL-AND-IP.md](LEGAL-AND-IP.md) is about how to do this legally — commission
original artwork, licence photography, or lean into brand-neutral "silhouette /
blueprint / your-own-photography" content. Do not skip it.
