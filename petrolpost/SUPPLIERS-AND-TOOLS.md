# Suppliers & Tools — Revhead Post (Australia)

*Starting points, not endorsements. Get 2–3 quotes for anything you'll buy
repeatedly, and always order a physical sample before committing.*

---

## Printing (the core of your product)

You need short-run, high-quality printing of prints, cards and letters. Two
routes:

### A. Local Australian print shops (best for quality control & samples)
- **Officeworks Print & Copy** — cheap, everywhere, good for prototyping your
  first samples fast. <https://www.officeworks.com.au/print>
- **Snap Print / Kwik Kopy / Minuteman Press** — franchise print shops in most
  cities; good for short runs of prints and cards.
- **Local trading-card / photo printers** — search "custom trading card printing
  Australia" and "giclée art print Australia" for the artwork tier.
- **Photo prints (the Polaroid-style items)** — Harvey Norman Photos, Officeworks
  Photos, or a lab like Fujifilm/Kodak Express for authentic photo stock.

### B. Print-on-demand / trade printers (best at scale)
- **Trade printers** (e.g. CMYKhub, Ovoo, Whirlwind Print — trade/wholesale)
  for larger runs once volumes justify it.
- **International card printers** (MakePlayingCards / MPC, Printerstudio) for
  proper trading cards — long lead times, so plan ahead and factor freight.

> **Tip:** Standardise every drop to the same paper sizes (e.g. all A5) and card
> stock so you can batch-print and negotiate a flat per-drop price.

## Packaging & mailers

- **Rigid envelopes / board-backed mailers** — keeps prints flat in the post.
  Suppliers: **Packworld**, **Better Packaging Co** (recycled/compostable),
  **eBox**, **Signet**, or bulk on **Amazon AU** to start.
- **Branded stickers / seals / tissue** — **Sticker Mule**, **Vistaprint AU**,
  or a local sticker printer.
- **Keep total weight ≤125g** to stay in the Australia Post Large-Letter band.

## Shipping / fulfilment

- **Australia Post — MyPost Business** (start here): cheaper letter rates,
  bulk labels, tracking options. <https://auspost.com.au/business>
- Design to the **Large Letter** dimensions (≤260×360mm, ≤20mm thick, ≤125g)
  for the lowest postage. Confirm current price:
  <https://auspost.com.au/sending/send-within-australia/letters>
- **Outsourced pick-and-pack** once you pass ~200 subs: search "subscription box
  fulfilment Australia" — providers like **Bezos.ai**, **Shippit** partners, or
  local 3PLs. Until then, fulfil from home.

## Design & content tools

- **Canva Pro** (~$18/mo) — fastest way to design beautiful prints/cards solo.
  <https://www.canva.com>
- **Adobe Illustrator / Photoshop** — if you want full control (blueprints,
  vector art).
- **Original artwork** — commission illustrators on **Behance**, **Dribbble**,
  local art schools, or car-artist communities. Owning the art outright solves
  most of your IP problem (see [LEGAL-AND-IP.md](LEGAL-AND-IP.md)).
- **Stock/licensed car photography** — reputable libraries where you *pay for a
  licence*: Getty/iStock, Shutterstock, Adobe Stock. Read each licence for
  "editorial vs commercial" and print/merchandise rights.
- **AI-generated imagery** — usable for *original, brand-neutral* art, but do
  **not** use it to imitate a specific marque's protected badges/trade dress.

## Marketing & ops tools

- **Email/marketing:** Klaviyo (integrates with Shopify; free tier) or Mailchimp.
- **Link-in-bio:** the Instagram bio link is your funnel — point it at the
  landing page / waitlist. Use Shopify's own link or a simple linktree.
- **Analytics:** Shopify analytics + Meta/TikTok pixels for ads later.
- **Bookkeeping:** spreadsheet → **Xero** (~$35/mo) as you grow.
- **Scheduling social:** Later, Buffer, or Meta Business Suite (free).

---

## Recommended minimal starting stack

1. **Shopify Basic** + **Seal Subscriptions** (free) — store & recurring billing.
2. **Officeworks / local printer** — samples and first small runs.
3. **Rigid mailers from a packaging supplier** — protect the prints.
4. **Australia Post MyPost Business** — cheap Large-Letter postage.
5. **Canva Pro** — design everything.
6. **Klaviyo free tier** — capture the waitlist and email subscribers.

Total tooling: roughly **$70–115/month** + per-unit COGS (see
[FINANCIAL-MODEL.md](FINANCIAL-MODEL.md)).
