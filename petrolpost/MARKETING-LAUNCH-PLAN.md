# Marketing & Launch Plan — Getting the First 100 Subscribers

*The product sells itself if the right car people see it. Your job is
distribution. For a visual, nostalgic, giftable product, the channel is
**short-form video** + a **pre-launch waitlist**.*

---

## Phase 0 — Before you launch (build demand first)

1. **Publish the landing page** ([`site/index.html`](site/index.html)) with an
   email capture. Point your Instagram bio link at it (that's exactly what the
   `?utm_source=ig&utm_medium=social&utm_content=link_in_bio` link in the
   original brief is doing).
2. **Start the socials now** — @revheadpost on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
   Shorts. Reserve the handles even if the store isn't live.
3. **Build a waitlist** of at least 200–500 emails before you open sales. Offer
   the **Founders' Edition** (first 100 subscribers, numbered, locked-in price)
   as the incentive to join early.

---

## The content engine (your #1 growth driver)

Car content performs extremely well on short-form video. Post **4–7×/week**.
Batch-film so it's sustainable. Winning formats:

- **"Guess the car" reveals** — mirror your product's surprise mechanic on
  video. Tease a silhouette/blueprint, then reveal.
- **Unboxing** — film every monthly drop being opened. This is your hero format;
  it shows the product *and* the surprise.
- **"The story behind…"** — 30–60s on why a legendary car matters (Bathurst
  winners, the Skyline GT-R, the Falcon GT, the 911). Storytelling = your brand.
- **Design process** — timelapse of a print/blueprint/trading card being made.
- **Nostalgia bait** — "If you remember this car, you're officially old." Comment
  goldmine.
- **Australian angle** — Summernats, Bathurst, local meets, ute culture, the
  Holden-vs-Ford rivalry. Lean into it; it's your differentiator over the UK
  original.

**Hook in the first 1–2 seconds, caption for silent viewing, one clear CTA
("link in bio to join the waitlist").**

## Channels, in priority order

1. **Instagram Reels + TikTok** — primary. Organic reach is highest here for
   visual niches.
2. **YouTube Shorts + a long-form channel later** — car YouTube is huge and
   evergreen; long-form builds authority.
3. **Facebook groups** — Australian car-enthusiast and classic-car groups are
   large and active. Participate genuinely; don't spam.
4. **Reddit** — r/cars, r/CarsAustralia, marque-specific subs. Share, don't sell.
5. **Email** — nurture the waitlist; launch and monthly "this month's drop"
   reveals drive renewals and referrals.
6. **Paid ads (later)** — only once you have organic proof and a converting
   landing page. Start with a small Meta/TikTok retargeting budget.

---

## Partnerships & community

- **Car photographers & illustrators** — collaborate; solves content *and*
  supply (see legal doc). Feature them, credit them, licence their work.
- **Car clubs & events** — Summernats, Motorclassica, local cars-and-coffee.
  Offer club discounts; set up a stall.
- **Micro-influencers** — car-page accounts with 10k–100k followers will often
  post for a free subscription or small fee. Far better ROI than big names.
- **Gifting angle** — market hard into **Father's Day (Sept in AU)** and
  **Christmas**; "the gift for the petrolhead who has everything." Push the
  prepaid 3/6-month gift tiers.

---

## Referral & retention

- **Referral:** "Give a month, get a month" — subscribers refer friends for a
  free drop. Cheap, and referred subs churn less.
- **Retention mechanic:** the **trading-card set** — nobody wants a gap in their
  collection. Show the growing set in every unboxing.
- **Member perks:** vote on future cars, member-only back-issues, a private
  community group, early access to limited drops.
- **Win-back:** email lapsed subscribers with the car they'd have missed.

## Launch sequence (concrete)

| When | Action |
|------|--------|
| T-6 weeks | Landing page live; socials created; start posting; open waitlist. |
| T-4 weeks | Post first unboxing of a *sample* drop; push waitlist hard. |
| T-2 weeks | Email waitlist: "Founders' Edition opens in 14 days, first 100 only." |
| Launch day | Open Founders' pre-orders to waitlist first, then public. |
| +1 week | Public launch push; collaborate with 3–5 micro-influencers. |
| Month 1 | Ship first drop; film real customer unboxings; collect testimonials. |
| Ongoing | Monthly reveal content; gift pushes at Father's Day & Christmas. |

---

## What "good" looks like early

- **Waitlist → paid conversion:** 5–15% of a warm waitlist is a reasonable
  target for the first drop.
- **Monthly churn:** aim under 5–7%.
- **CAC via organic:** effectively your time — keep it that way as long as you
  can before paying for ads.
- **The metric that matters:** net active subscribers, growing every month.
