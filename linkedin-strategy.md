# QEH/2026 LinkedIn Recruitment Strategy

## Executive Overview
**Goal**: Attract high-performing recruiters to the Agency in 90 Days program through engaging, authentic LinkedIn content that speaks to their pain points and aspirations.

**Target Audience**: 
- High-performing recruiters (5+ years experience)
- Agency owners looking to optimize operations
- Recruiters considering independence
- Recruitment team leaders

---

## Strategy Pillars

### 1. **Aspiration & Freedom** 
Show what's possible when you own your desk. 90% billings, entrepreneurial control, no employee constraints.

### 2. **Proof & Results**
Real numbers: $50k/mo average run-rate in 90 days, 90% billings vs 15-20% as employee, rapidly growing Australian startup.

### 3. **Education & Insights**
Position QEH as thought leader in independent recruitment. Share recruitment trends, agency scaling lessons, business ownership mindset shifts.

### 4. **Community & Belonging**
Highlight the peer network, weekly masterminds, mentorship. Show that you're joining something real, not going solo.

### 5. **Transparency & Trust**
No hype. Pressure-test calls, clear requirements (experienced recruiters only), honest about what works and what's hard.

---

## Content Pillars (Weekly Rotation)

### Pillar A: **"The Math"** (Earnings/Independence)
- Billings comparison: Employee vs Owner
- Real case studies: [Name], a recruiter, went from X to Y
- Hidden costs of corporate recruitment
- What 90% really means (take-home examples)

**Post frequency**: 1-2x week
**Engagement driver**: Poll ("What would you do with 50% more take-home?")

### Pillar B: **"The Craft"** (Recruitment Tips + Scaling)
- Sourcing secrets that independent operators use
- Recruitment market insights
- How to build repeatable, scalable processes
- AI sourcing + ATS automation wins

**Post frequency**: 2-3x week
**Engagement driver**: Question ("What's your secret sauce?"), educational carousel

### Pillar C: **"The Community"** (Peer Support, Wins)
- Mastermind highlights, peer wins
- Testimonials from current operators
- Behind-the-scenes of the program
- Q&A from the community

**Post frequency**: 1-2x week
**Engagement driver**: User-generated content (UGC), taglines, story-telling

### Pillar D: **"The Call"** (Conversion-Focused)
- Direct CTAs to book strategy calls
- "Why now?" – timing arguments for going independent
- Objection busting: "I'm too comfortable", "It's too risky", "I don't know where to start"
- Limited spots messaging (if genuine)

**Post frequency**: 1x week
**Engagement driver**: Urgency, specificity, social proof

---

## Post Formats

### Format 1: **Contrast Posts** 
"Employee vs Owner" comparisons. Visual: two columns, emoji indicators.
```
👷 Employee Recruiter:
- 15-20% of billings
- No brand control
- Admin overhead
- Fixed hours
- Manager approval for everything

🎯 Independent Operator:
- 90% of billings
- Your brand, your rules
- Admin handled by us
- Your schedule
- Direct client relationships
```

### Format 2: **Story/Case Study**
"Meet [Operator]. 3 months ago they were an employee. Today they're billing $50k/mo under their own brand."

### Format 3: **Stat Posts**
Single stat with context: "$50k/mo. That's the average run-rate our operators hit in their first 90 days. And they keep 90% of it."

### Format 4: **Question Polls**
"Quick poll: What's holding you back from running your own recruitment desk? [Options: Fear, Logistics, Lack of support, Timing, Other]"

### Format 5: **Educational Carousel**
"5 Things Independent Recruiters Know That Employees Don't" (5-slide carousel)

### Format 6: **Call-to-Action**
"If you're a high-performing recruiter considering your next move, book a 30-min strategy call. We'll pressure-test your numbers and show you the real path to 90%."

## Engagement Mechanics

### To Maximize Reach:
- **First comment strategy**: Reply to your own post with a value-add or question within 2 hours of posting
- **Engagement cadence**: Post at 8am AEST Tuesday-Thursday (peak professional hours)
- **CTA placement**: Always include clear, specific CTA (not generic "connect with me")
- **Hashtag strategy**: Mix broad (#recruitment, #entrepreneurship) + niche (#independentrecruiter, #recruitmentoperator)
- **Video preference**: 20% of posts as video (testimonials, founder insight, market commentary)

### Visual Consistency:
- Color palette: QEH brand colors (derive from site)
- Font: Professional, readable at mobile size
- Sizing: 1200x627px for image posts (optimal LinkedIn ratio)
- Watermark: Subtle QEH/2026 branding

## Measurement & Optimization

### KPIs to Track:
1. **Engagement Rate**: Target 3-5% (comments + likes + shares)
2. **Click-Through Rate**: Target 1-2% to strategy call booking
3. **Profile Views**: Baseline + 20% growth target
4. **Strategy Call Bookings**: Track which posts/pillars drive conversions
5. **Video Views**: Track play-through rate (stop when % drops below 30s)

### Optimization Loop:
- Weekly: Review top 3 posts, identify patterns
- Bi-weekly: Adjust posting times, format mix
- Monthly: Full calendar review, strategy pivot if needed
